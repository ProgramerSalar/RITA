# Rita Educational Video Dataset

## Dataset Description

The **Rita** is a large-scale, curated collection of educational videos designed to advance research in multimodal learning, video understanding, and educational technology.

- **Homepage:** [Link to a project page if it exists, otherwise remove]



### Overview

This dataset contains approximately `100+` hours of educational content across `1 Lack` videos, totaling ~100 GB. The videos cover a wide range of academic subjects and skill levels, from `class 1 math subject` to `class 8 math subject`.

### Supported Tasks

* **Video-Language Modeling:** Training models to understand the relationship between visual content and spoken narration.
* **Automatic Lecture Transcription (ASR):** Converting speech in educational contexts to text.
* **Educational Video Classification:** Categorizing videos by subject, difficulty level, or teaching style.
* **Knowledge Tracing & Summarization:** Extracting key concepts and creating summaries from video content.
* **Multimodal Embodiment:** Learning joint representations of video, and text.
